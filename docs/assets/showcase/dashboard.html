1 · Season overview

LAI drives canopy absorption, Rin drives illumination, and their product (scaled by fqe) controls the proxy SIF signal. The three curves share the same time axis so the seasonal coupling is immediately visible.

3 · Radiation partitioning

Total shortwave (Rin) is split into direct beam and diffuse sky components using the BRL clearness-index model. The diffuse fraction controls how much light penetrates deep into the canopy.

↓

4 · Proxy SIF calibration

The proxy model generates a SIF-like signal from the canopy and radiation inputs. The optimiser adjusts fqe (fluorescence quantum efficiency) from 0.0080 to 0.0179 to match the noisy synthetic observations, reducing RMSE from 0.1077 to 0.00254.
